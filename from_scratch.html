Recent Winner Highlights

  • Masters 2026 - Rory McIlroy
    • This win made McIlroy a back-to-back champion. After winning the Masters for the first time in 2025 and securing a Grand Slam, Rory became one of four men to win two consecutive Masters.

  • Masters 2021 - Hideki Matsuyama
    • After becoming the first Japanese winner of the Masters, his caddie, Shota Hayafuji, took the flag from the flagstick on the 18th green and bowed in respect toward the course.

  • Masters 2019 - Tiger Woods
    • 2019 marked Tiger's 5th win at Augusta. This set Woods as the second-highest repeat winner of the Masters Tournament. More impressively, this 2019 win was a comeback. After undergoing multiple back surgeries, Tiger came back to prove himself as what most consider the greatest golfer of all time.

  • Masters 1997 - Tiger Woods
    • This was Tiger's first win at the Masters. This was historical not only for his career, but also the PGA as a whole, as Woods became the first African-American to win a major.
